Who's Still Talking About Guantanamo Bay?

The past ten years of news stories show declining coverage of the topic by the popular press, while a small number of people remain in detention.

Spring 2020




Since the early 1990's, scores of refugees and suspected terrorists have been detained at Guantanamo Bay. In 2009, President Obama signed an executive order to close Guantanamo Bay within a year. More than a decade later, forty individuals are still being held inside the prison. In response to the continued operation of Guantanamo Bay as a detention facility, I investigated how the media has covered the topic over the past decade.
